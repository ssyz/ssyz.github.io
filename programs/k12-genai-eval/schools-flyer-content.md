# OurDojo K-12 AI Evaluation Learning Sprint

## Make AI purchasing decisions with evidence, not marketing claims

1,000 samples · 1 evaluator · $1,000–$1,500 · Ready in 2 weeks

## Who This is For
Built for curriculum directors, procurement teams, and IT leads evaluating AI edtech tools before purchase or renewal. If vendors are making claims about learning outcomes, grade-level alignment, or AI accuracy — and you have no independent way to verify them — this is for you.

## How It Works

1. **Scoping Call** — 30-min meeting to identify the AI tool you want evaluated, agree on evaluator, sample method, and grade levels
2. **1,000-Sample Evaluation** — The tool's AI content evaluated against research-verified frameworks
3. **Deliverables** — Report, raw data, failure heatmap within 2 weeks
4. **Findings Walkthrough** — 1-hour meeting to review results and make clear recommendations
