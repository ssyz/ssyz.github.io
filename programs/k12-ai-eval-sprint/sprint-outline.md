## What's Included

### 1. Scoping Call (30 min)
Agree on evaluator selection, sample collection method, and target grade levels/subjects.

### 2. 1,000-Sample Evaluation
Client selects one [Learning Commons evaluator](https://docs.learningcommons.org/evaluators/understanding-evaluators/about-evaluators):

- **Grade Level Appropriateness** — Is generated content at the right reading level?
- **Sentence Structure Complexity** — Is sentence complexity appropriate for the target grade?
- **Vocabulary Complexity** — Are word choices accessible for the intended audience?
- **Subject Matter Knowledge** — Does the text assume appropriate prior knowledge?

### 3. Deliverables

**Baseline Evaluation Report** (core deliverable):
- Executive summary with headline accuracy metrics
- Detailed error analysis: failure rates by subject, topic, grade band
- Failure mode breakdown: direction of misses (too hard vs. too easy), severity distribution, systematic patterns
- Annotated examples: 5–10 hand-selected samples with full evaluator analysis
- Diagnostic insights: specific, actionable observations about what's causing failures
- Product improvement roadmap: prioritized quick wins based on findings

**Raw evaluation data (CSV)** — full dataset with all metadata for engineering teams to debug prompts

**Failure mode heatmap (1-page visual)** — failure rate mapped by subject x topic x grade band

### 4. Findings Walkthrough Call (30 min)
Walk through the report, answer questions, discuss next steps.
