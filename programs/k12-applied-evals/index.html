Frequently Asked Questions

Is the evaluation public or private?

Partners can choose how findings are shared, with options for public summaries or private reports.

What is OurDojo?

OurDojo builds independent evaluation infrastructure for AI in education. Our goal is to make claims about AI quality testable, comparable, and decision-relevant. We bring structured benchmarking methodologies, real-world product analysis, and years of recognized engineering and data science expertise. See our initial research at ourdojo.org/research.

How independent is the evaluation?

OurDojo operates as a third-party evaluator with transparent methodologies. Methodology will be open sourced, so schools and district leaders can verify evaluation credibility.

Can results be used in marketing or fundraising?

Yes. Partners receive narrative assets designed for external credibility.

What if results are unfavorable?

The program includes iteration cycles to support improvement before public positioning.

How is this different from efficacy research?

Traditional efficacy studies like RCTs can take 12–36 months and cost upwards of $150,000–$500,000. OurDojo evaluations are designed to deliver actionable product quality insights within weeks, not years, at a fraction of the cost. The focus is on near-term product and procurement decisions, not long-horizon academic research.

What types of products qualify?

AI-enabled tools designed for K-12 instructional or operational use.
