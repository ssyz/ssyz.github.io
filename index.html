OurDojo

Ensuring innovations in education technology amplify humanity, rather than replace it.

The Problem

We are losing the desire to learn. Today’s tools make it easier to avoid thinking than to engage with it. Instead, people of all ages are outsourcing: from running every email through GPT to using AI to cheat on homework. This is new. And it’s spreading fast.

Why It Matters

Curiosity is what pulls us toward learning; and learning is what keeps us curious. When that cycle breaks, our inner world stops growing. A society that no longer learns becomes less empathetic and more depressed. The result is a world that is more fearful, more disconnected, and less human. OurDojo exists to make the world more curious and empathetic.
