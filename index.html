Our Mission

OurDojo is an applied AI evaluations company. We are a team of scientists and engineers who are committed to rigorous evaluation and evidence-based analysis of AI technologies. We care deeply about the future of our vast, diverse, and shared education ecosystem that ultimately defines society.

How We Work

01 Extreme Craft

Treat everything as a first-class product, especially the invisible parts. Evaluation frameworks, documentation, and internal tools should feel as deliberate and well-designed as a luxury good. If it touches learning, trust, or truth, it deserves excellence.

02 Context and Evidence, Not Control

We hire unusually responsible people and give them radically transparent context. Decisions live closest to the work, not the hierarchy. We default to evidence-based decision-making and rigorous analytical practices, especially when claims affect learners.

03 Stay Lean, Stay Sharp

A small team of mission-aligned experts, moving with urgency and taste, will out-innovate the market. We optimize for leverage, not headcount. We default to GSD. We are digital by design: remote-friendly, async-heavy, documentation-driven. Deep work beats performative busyness.

Why It Matters

Learning is what keeps us curious; and curiosity is what pulls us toward learning. When that cycle breaks, our perspective narrows, our creativity diminishes, and we stop growing. A society that no longer learns becomes less empathetic and more depressed. The result is a world that's more fearful, more disconnected, and less human.
